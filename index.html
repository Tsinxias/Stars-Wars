The Debut

The story begins in the center of Belgium...Brussels.

The little chinese girl debuted a journey of nothing but new techniques of coding and web developpement. Her background was nothing but marketing stuff.

Everything she did turned out ok but not so great. So she challenged herself to do more and more work to reach the sunny side of the coding.

A wall stood up in front of her, will the success await at the end of this road...
